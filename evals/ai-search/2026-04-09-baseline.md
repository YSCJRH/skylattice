# 2026-04-09 Discoverability Baseline

## Run Metadata

- date: 2026-04-09
- review model: four isolated perspectives
- agents: English discovery, Chinese discovery, technical indexing review, external authority scout
- public surfaces reviewed: GitHub repo, GitHub Pages site, release pages, `robots.txt`, `sitemap.xml`, `llms.txt`, and the current public proof surfaces

## Summary Metrics

- English non-brand discoverability: `3/10`
- Chinese non-brand discoverability: `2/10`
- citation readiness / technical packaging: `7/10`
- official Pages citation rate: low but technically possible
- snippet readability in Chinese: blocked by mojibake before this optimization round

## Key Findings

- the public Pages and repository packaging were already good enough to be cited, but not yet strong enough to surface naturally for non-brand queries
- Chinese public pages were unreadable, which directly hurt snippet quality and Chinese discoverability
- the absence of a non-pre-release stable release weakened authority and `latest release` signals
- external authority signals remained thin even though the internal proof surfaces were solid

## Actions Triggered By This Baseline

- rewrite the Chinese mirror in clean UTF-8
- tighten the English answer pages around search-shaped questions
- prepare a stable non-pre-release `v0.2.1` release surface
- prepare launch, comparison, and community distribution assets
- switch to a weekly isolated-agent review loop instead of relying on one conversational search surface
